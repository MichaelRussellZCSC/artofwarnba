PUTTING A PRICE ON FRIENDSHIP

One of the best parts of playing AoW is joining the Discord servers run by the players which are loosely tied to the game.   Join and you will get why so many play.  The community feels like family and the members have become friends.

I am deleting the game though.  

 The 3.36 upload requires players, who want to be in the top 500, to spend  $5,000 - $10,000 or they risk all the money they have spent  up to this point to become virtually worthless and will drop to the lower tiers of the game.  

I get the business side of the game but I have already spent nearly 2 grand on the game and it hit me that there is no end to the money grabs.  I realised if I didn't spend another several thousand dollars on this upload I would be forced out of clan I am in to make way for someone else who would and I realised the are trying to monetize my friendships to a level that is crazy and I needed to leave the insanity they create because it isn't normal.

I don't mind spending on a mobile game as a service with such a great community but the devs shouldn't create a game which requires players to spend 5K-10K a year to stay in a clan of their choice.
