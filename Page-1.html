Art Of War: Legions

Is The Game Fun?

It can be.  

The best part about the game though is the Discord server the players have built  which is pretty awesome.  The  community feels more like family at times.

I am deleting the game though.  

 This last upload the devs will require 5-10K US Dollars for players to be in the top 200.  I am not exaggerating the cost to play this mobile game.  It is crazy.  

So, after 5 months of being an active player, I am out.  They are trying to monetize the Discord servers that the players have built for their own profit which is wrong.

There has to be other games out there that isn't trying to rip players off like this.

I will use this page to keep you posted on my journey.
